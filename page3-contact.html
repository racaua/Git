Environment and Safety

We take care of Ireland's nature on every single flight. Here are some common questions about our impact on the environment:

Question Our Answer
Do birds get hurt? No. Balloons are very quiet. Birds fly away naturally. Zero incidents in 20 years.
Are balloons polluting? Very low emissions. We use clean propane burners and offset our carbon footprint.
Do we disturb wildlife? No. Our pilots avoid nesting areas and follow wildlife-safe routes at all times.
Is landing damaging? Landing areas are chosen carefully. We always get landowner permission first.
